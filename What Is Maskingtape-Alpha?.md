## 🔍 Quick Summary

- **Maskingtape-alpha** is an experimental AI image model spotted in testing environments  
- Likely an early version of a next-gen system (possibly GPT-Image-2)  
- Biggest improvement: **accurate text rendering inside images**  
- Not publicly available yet  

> In short: early-stage, but unusually promising.

---

## 🚨 Why This Model Is Getting Attention

Most AI image tools still struggle with:

- ❌ Broken or unreadable text  
- ❌ Inconsistent layouts  
- ❌ Visually correct but logically flawed scenes  

Maskingtape-alpha appears to reduce these issues in a noticeable way.

Instead of just improving aesthetics, it improves **usability**—especially for real workflows like design and UI prototyping.
